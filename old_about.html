Hi, I’m Kasey McCormick, a designer, developer, and learner. I’m enamored with following an idea from conception to execution and with every step in between.

I obtained my Bachelor’s of Studio Arts in Graphic Design and a minor in Marketing from University of Nebraska at Omaha. I earned a development certificate from Omaha Code School, where I learned full stack development, and learned Design Thinking facilitation from IBM’s training. I have always enjoyed learning new skills and pick them up in various ways. I’m passionate about making experiences better in the digital or analog world. When I’m not on my computer creating I’m often outside or painting while listening to audio books or podcasts.
